What is Water My Plants?

Water My Plants is a web application that helps you manage and organise your plant watering schedule. The application supports multiple schedules to be set up across different plants and will alert you via text when it is time to get watering.

How much does Water My Plants cost?

Water My Plants is completely free of charge, built by gardeners for gardeners. As such, we'd love to here your opinions so that we can continue to improve the usefullness of our app.
